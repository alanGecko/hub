Quadrilaterals

Quadrilaterals are 4 sided polygons.

Types
Kite Two pairs of equal length sides.
Trapezoid/Trapezium A trapezium is a quadrilateral with no parallel sides. A trapezoid is a quadrilateral with opposite parallel sides. Also in the UK a trapezoid is called a trapezium and a trapezium is a trapezoid.
Parallelogram A parallelogram has opposite sides parallel and equal in length and opposite angles are equal.
Rhombus A rhombus is a four-sided shape where all sides have equal length, it's kinda like a tilted square.
Square A square has all equal sides and 90 degree angles. It has two sets of parallel sides.
Rectangle A rectangle is a four-sided shape where every angle is a right angle (90Â°). It has two sets of parallel sides.
